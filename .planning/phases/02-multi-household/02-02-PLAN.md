<objective>
Flip the application from single-household to active-household at the one central seam (the household resolver + tRPC context), enable multi-membership, rewrite recipe scoping to use recipes.household_id, and assign imported/created recipes to the active cookbook.

Purpose: Delivers HOUSE-01..05,07. The active-household resolver makes member-scoped secondary repos (groceries/calendar/allergies/caldav) follow the active cookbook for free; the recipe-visibility rewrite + creation-assignment delivers per-cookbook recipes.
Output: New/changed repositories, tRPC context/middleware, households router (list + switchActive), recipe creation/import wiring, queue payloads carrying householdId, and updated household DTO/zod.
</objective>
