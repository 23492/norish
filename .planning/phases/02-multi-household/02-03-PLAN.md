<objective>
Enforce the per-cookbook visibility boundary server-side in permissions.ts and the recipe helpers, then prove it with dedicated isolation tests at both the DB-scoping and tRPC levels. This is the security-critical core of the phase (HOUSE-06).

Purpose: Guarantees one household can never see/edit/delete another household's recipes, independent of the active selection. Tests are the deliverable — the boundary must be regression-proof.
Output: Reworked permission checks (signature carries recipe householdId + requester member household ids), updated recipe helpers, and new/extended isolation test suites; existing test-utils updated to construct the new ctx fields.
</objective>
