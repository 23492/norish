This plan mirrors that: guarantee the two DeepSeek V4 ids are always offered in the admin model picker (merged with â€” and de-duped against â€” any live results), so a user can pick them without typing.

SCOPE GUARD: do NOT re-architect the factory/listing; do NOT touch the enum (deepseek already present); do NOT add a dependency; do NOT add an env var or any key. Smallest change that makes the v4 ids selectable + a unit test proving the dispatch + listing behaviour.
